MANAGE YOUR RISK
  • You and your colleagues will always have access to the Konsileo Risk Hub
  • In addition to managing your insurances this will provide a series of additional apps to capture risk based data about assets, vehicles, drivers and health and safety to provide authoritative data sources and to streamline your risk management processes
  • The Risk Hub will also provide you with an enterprise standard holistic risk management framework, that you can embed in the governance of your business so that you can manage all the risks (both insurable and non-insurable) in one place.
