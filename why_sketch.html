Why Sketch


by @nomadiccreature

I read awhile back in a medium blog “Designing in Sketch is like using a simplified version of illustrator with the layer management of Photoshop.” Here’s my personal experience. You can do all things in a single platform

  • Quick and dirty prototype with highest standards quickly - Only paper and powerpoint are next to this.
  • Comes with in-built easy to transferrable assets across cross platform (Artboards), also makes it easy to transfer and test in mockup tools like invisionapp etc.
  • Easy to learn: A design tool that actually a 5 year could use and still have fun :)
  • Pixel-perfect
  • Many different formats could be generated as well as SVG are very well rendered
  • Not at all expensive and if you are a Beta user its lifetime free with some hacks ;)
  • Becoming an industry standard, next time you hire someone they are expected to know sketch or at least they can learn it much faster
  • Light in your harddisk.
  • A huge community for a startup like Sketch that is just few years old
  • You can directly transfer your Sketch artboards to Invisionapp. See how
