HOW WE WORK
1. MEET BROKER
  1. Uses the proprietary Konsileo Fact Find process to really get to know your business in your language
  2. Captures this using state of the art tech including video, photos, geo-location etc to give the most comprehensive pic to underwriters of the risks you face
  3. Ensures that positive risk management features are properly articulated and captured
  4. Gives you instant access to your data for you to view and share with colleagues in compliance with The Insurance Act 2015

2. GO TO MARKET
  1. Broker speaks to insurers on your behalf armed with the best quality risk submission that the underwriters will see from anyone in the market
